# Privacy Statement

## Summary

Speed Demon is designed to be privacy-first.

**Speed Demon does not collect any personal data.**

## What We Collect

We do not collect, track, transmit, or sell user data.

- No analytics collection
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party tracking SDKs
- No account system
- No server-side profile data

## Location and Trip Data

Speed Demon uses on-device location data only to provide app features such as:

- live speed and heading
- trip/session tracking
- trip history and route context

This data remains under your control on your device and, if enabled by you, your personal iCloud account for sync.

## Data Sharing

Speed Demon does not sell or share personal data with data brokers or advertisers.

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If iCloud sync is enabled, trip data is stored in your private CloudKit container tied to your Apple ID.

- Data is managed by Apple’s iCloud/CloudKit privacy and security model.
- The developer does not receive or access your private CloudKit records.

## Import / Export

When you export trip data (for example JSON/CSV), the exported files are stored where you choose. You are responsible for how those files are handled after export.

## Security Reporting

If you believe you have found a security issue, please follow the process documented in `SECURITY.md`.

## Updates

This statement may be updated if app behavior changes. Any future changes will continue to prioritize user privacy.
